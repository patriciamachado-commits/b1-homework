1. Reading Comprehension

Maria's Daily Commute

Maria takes the subway to work every day. She catches the 7:30 train at the station near her house. The ride takes about 45 minutes.

Sometimes she misses the train and has to wait 15 minutes for the next one. When that happens, she gets stuck in rush hour traffic if she decides to call a cab instead.

On Fridays, Maria carpools with her coworker. They take turns driving. This saves money on gas and is better for the environment.

Maria thinks public transportation is cheaper and faster than driving during rush hour. She transfers once to get to her office building.

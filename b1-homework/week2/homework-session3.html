1. Reading Comprehension

Opening a Bank Account

When you want to open a bank account in the United States, there are some things you must know.

First, you must bring identification - a passport or driver's license. You should also bring proof of address, like a utility bill. You can choose between a checking account or a savings account.

With a checking account, you can write checks and use a debit card. You should keep track of your balance so you don't get overdraft fees. You shouldn't spend more money than you have!

A savings account is different. You can't write checks, but you can earn interest on your money. You should try to save a little money every month.

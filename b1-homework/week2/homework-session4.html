1. Reading Comprehension

Opening a Bank Account

When you want to open a bank account, you must bring identification - usually a passport or driver's license. You should also bring proof of address, like a utility bill.

You can choose between a checking account or a savings account. A checking account is good for daily expenses - you can write checks and use a debit card. A savings account is better if you want to earn interest.

You shouldn't share your PIN number with anyone. You must keep it secret to protect your money.

1. What must you bring to open an account?

Money only
Identification
A friend

2. What can you do with a checking account?

Earn high interest
Write checks and use a debit card
Get free money

3. What shouldn't you do with your PIN?

Remember it
Use it at ATMs
Share it with others

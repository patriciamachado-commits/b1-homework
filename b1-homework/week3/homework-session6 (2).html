A Day at the Flea Market

Last weekend, I went to a flea market. I saw a beautiful vintage lamp. The seller wanted $50 for it.

"Is that your best price?" I asked. The seller thought about it. "I could do $45," he said.

"How about $35?" I offered. He shook his head. "I can't go that low. The lowest I can go is $40."

"It's a deal!" I said, and we shook hands. I was happy - I saved $10 by bargaining!

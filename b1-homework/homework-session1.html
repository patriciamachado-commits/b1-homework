1. Reading Comprehension

Maria's Favorite Holiday

Last year, I celebrated Christmas with my family in Mexico. We prepared special food for weeks before the holiday. My mother cooked tamales, and my grandmother made her famous pozole soup.

On Christmas Eve, we went to church and then had a big dinner at midnight. The children opened their presents after dinner. Everyone sang traditional songs and danced until very late.

I loved this celebration because the whole family came together. We talked, laughed, and shared stories. It was the best Christmas ever!

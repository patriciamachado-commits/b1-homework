1. Reading Comprehension

Planning a Trip to Athens

My sisters and I are going to visit Greece next month. I have been so busy at work and I really need to get away. We are going to set off on our big adventure around 5:00 AM.

My husband is going to drop us off at the airport. Our flight will take off around 8:00 AM. It will be a long journey!

When we arrive, we are going to check in at a beautiful hotel near the Acropolis. After a brief rest, we will set out to find a restaurant for dinner and look around the city.

I think we will have an amazing time! It is going to be an awesome two weeks!

3. Writing

You and a friend are planning a dream vacation! Write a short paragraph (6-8 sentences) describing your travel plans. Think about these questions as you write:

  • Why do you need this vacation?
  • How are you getting to the airport? What time does your flight leave?
  • What will you do when you arrive at your destination?
  • Where will you stay? What places will you explore?
